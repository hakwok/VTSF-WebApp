Students

Sessions 3x a week, 1 hour each day, every Mon, Wed, Fri. Groups of 3-5 students.

Registration for the Summer 2023 semester is closed.

What We Provide
What We Provide

1. Class Prep
Our summer program will run from the week of June 12th to the week of August 4th, totaling 24 classes over the course of the summer. Classes will build on a students current understanding in preparation for the upcoming school year by teaching ahead. They will be prepared for the upcoming school year.



2. Tailored Learning
We hope to achieve mastery within the class before moving onto another topic, so they don't have gaps in their learning.

Topics
Mathematics
English
Grades 3-5 Math

In this early stage of math, we strive to build a soild foundation for your child. Our curriculum, which consists of addition, division, fractions, decimals, and more will result in a strong arithmetic foundation for future math.

Grades 3-5 English

In elementary English, we focus on grammar, such as sentence structures and tenses. We also teach vocabulary and reading comprehension to prepare your child for more complex texts.

Grades 6-8 Math

As their exploration of math continues, we solidify their understanding of interpreting graphs and data, include elements of geometry, and move on to the fundamentals of algebra.

Grades 6-8 English

We reinforce the core concepts of English, such as grammar and reading comprehension. We also teach students to read and write narratives, arguementitive essays, and more.

Algebra 1

We focus only on Algebra 1 topics, from linear eqautions to quardratics. We also include the manipulation of variables and various word problems.

Attendance Policy
Attendance Policy

We ask your child to not miss more than three sessions, as that will negatively affect your child's learning and may also take up a spot from another learner who could have attended. Message their tutor if they have an extenuating circumstance and must miss a session. If they have three unexcused absences, we may remove your child from their session.
