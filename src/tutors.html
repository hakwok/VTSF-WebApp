Tutors

Teach students in need and get volunteer hours.

Registration for the Summer 2023 semester is closed.

Program Details

Session Details
Tutors will work in small groups of 3 to 5 students on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays for 1 hour each class via Google Meets. Our summer program will run from the week of June 12th to the week of August 4th, totaling 24 classes over the course of the summer. Class resources and lesson plans are provided. You may teach more than one class.



Aim
The summer will be focused on helping students catch up and advance themselves for the upcoming school year. Tutors will teach course materials from provided lesson plans, ideally adapting to the learning speed of students to maximize engagement.



Expections
All tutors must attend a virtual mandatory training session before starting tutoring. During the semester, tutors are expected to attend all classes in a timely manner and have proper communication with parents if they are unable to attend. In addition, tutors will be required to complete weekly reflections and update class attendance, which will be discussed during the training.
